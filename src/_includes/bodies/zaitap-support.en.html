FAQ — Residents (Applicants)

Q. Is it really free?

Yes. Download, self-filing (XML generation, NFC signing, API submission) is free. Under the Administrative Scrivener Act, we do not collect fees from applicants. We may optionally offer IT features unrelated to applications, such as cloud storage and deadline notifications, in the future.

Q. Should I choose self-filing or scrivener consultation?

If the procedure is straightforward and you can handle it yourself, use self-filing. For complex cases (past residence history, company changes, correction history, etc.), you can consult an administrative scrivener after reviewing the fee estimate in the app. The estimate is shown before submitting a consultation request, and the request is sent only after your consent.

Q. Can the fee estimate differ from the final amount?

Yes. Estimates are reference information based on procedure type. The final fee is presented by the scrivener, and you pay the scrivener directly. We do not receive or mediate compensation.

Q. Do I need a My Number Card?

For online submission to the Residence Application Procedures API via self-filing, electronic signing with a My Number Card (or an IC card appropriate to the procedure, such as a residence card) via smartphone NFC is required.

FAQ — Administrative Scriveners

Q. How do I register?

Register your office name in the dedicated "ZaiTap for Administrative Scriveners" app and start with an annual subscription (App Store / Google Play). No invitation code is required.

Q. What client personal information can I see?

Only the consultation summary and encrypted documents uploaded by the client. You can decrypt and view them in the app. Summaries are designed to exclude PII where possible.

Q. What is a matching quota?

It is the number of consultation acceptances included in the annual plan. If you exceed the quota, additional credits (consumable IAP) must be purchased. Details are available in the app.
