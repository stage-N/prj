Why WBGT records matter

Quantify heat stroke risk with the Ministry of the Environment heat index standard.

Compliance with labor safety laws

For outdoor and indoor hot work, tracking and recording WBGT values is fundamental to safety management. This app links work records with WBGT values and supports up to 3 years of record keeping.

From paper logs to your phone

See current WBGT at a glance on a semicircle gauge. Plan work with a 24-hour forecast and get push alerts when thresholds are exceeded. Reduce supervisor burden and prevent missed warnings.

Everything you need for heat safety on the job site, in one app.
