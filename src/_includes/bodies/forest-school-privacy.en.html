Privacy Policy

STAGEN Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") respects user privacy in Forest School (the "App") and is committed to protecting personal information.

We do not collect any personal information in this App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information.

  • No account registration is required
  • No network communication during gameplay
  • We do not use analytics tools or advertising SDKs
  • No access to location, camera, or microphone

When using app updates (Expo Updates), an internet connection may be used temporarily to fetch update data. Game progress data is not sent externally.

2. Children's Privacy

The App is designed primarily for children ages 3–5 (in line with COPPA and similar children's privacy principles). We do not collect personal information from children. Game data is stored on the device only.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Only the following data is stored in local storage on the device and is not sent externally:

  • Game progress (number of stars, unlocked areas, companion collection, etc.)
  • Forest Stamps (daily play records)
  • Difficulty and play count per game
  • Parent settings (language, sound, screen orientation, play time limits, etc.)

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

Because the App does not collect personal information, there is no disclosure to third parties.

5. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be changed without prior notice. The revised policy takes effect when posted on this page.
