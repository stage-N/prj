Privacy Policy

STAGEN ("we," "us," or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of personal information in Heat Stroke Alert (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App uses your device's location to obtain WBGT (Wet Bulb Globe Temperature) values.

  • Location is used only to obtain WBGT values
  • Coordinates are sent only when requesting weather data from the Open-Meteo API
  • No user registration is required

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device in a SQLite database and is not sent to our servers:

  • Activity records (start/end times, activity type, etc.)
  • Personal profile (age group, medical history — optional settings)
  • Notification and threshold settings
  • Data linking WBGT values to records

Threshold adjustments based on personal profile are not medical advice. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

3. External Communications

  • Open-Meteo API — Sends coordinates (latitude/longitude) to obtain weather data
  • Ministry of the Environment Heat Index site — Retrieves reference heat index data

4. Advertising

The App displays Google AdMob App Open ads when launching and when returning from the background. Google may collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, and similar information for ad delivery.

  • Advertising SDK used: Google AdMob
  • On iOS, we may request tracking permission for more relevant ads (declining does not affect app use or ad display)
  • To limit ad personalization, use your device's ad settings
  • Ads are not shown during heat stroke warnings

5. Notifications

The App provides local push notifications. Notifications are scheduled on your device; no external server is involved.

6. Analytics

The App does not use analytics tools.

7. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose activity records or WBGT data to third parties. For ad delivery only, information necessary for display may be provided to Google.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we revise this policy, we will announce the change in advance on GitHub.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
