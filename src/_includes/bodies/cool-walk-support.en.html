FAQ

Q. How is it different from WBGT Monitor?

Cool Walk suggests whether you can go out now, rather than just showing WBGT numbers. It provides go/no-go guidance and recommended times based on activity type.

Q. What is the basis for recommendations?

We combine WBGT (heat index), UV index, and PM2.5 to calculate safe time windows for each activity type (walking, running, cycling, outdoor work).

Q. Is this medical advice?

No. Cool Walk is not a medical device. It provides reference information for going outdoors based on WBGT, UV, and air quality. Consult a physician or other qualified professional for heat stroke prevention and health decisions.

Q. What is a shaded route? Do I need to enter a destination?

No. Starting from your current location, we automatically select parks, forests, and green spaces (OpenStreetMap) within 1.5 km and calculate walking routes within 15 minutes to each center. Among multiple candidates, we prioritize routes that pass near green areas (within 40 m). Building shadows and solar radiation forecasts are not included. Use as reference information only.

Q. Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored only on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
