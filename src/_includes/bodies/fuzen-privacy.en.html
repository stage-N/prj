Privacy Policy

STAGEN Inc. ("we", "us") respects your privacy in Fuzen ("the App") and is committed to protecting your personal information.

We do not collect personally identifiable information through the App.

1. Information we collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information.

  • No account registration required
  • Your code and learning progress are not sent to external servers
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs
  • No access to location, camera, or microphone

When using app updates (Expo Updates), a temporary internet connection may be used to fetch update data.

2. Data stored on your device

The following data is stored only on your device locally and is never transmitted externally:

  • Lesson progress
  • REPL input history
  • Code saved in My Library (with Full unlock purchase)
  • App settings (language, display preferences, etc.)

3. In-app purchases

Full unlock purchases are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive payment details such as credit card numbers. Purchase restoration is tied to your store account.

4. Third-party disclosure

Because we do not collect personally identifiable information, we do not disclose data to third parties.

5. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us via the Support page.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated without prior notice. The revised policy takes effect when posted on this page.

Effective: June 30, 2026
