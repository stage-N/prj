FAQ

Q. What is the difference between the free version and Pro?

The free version includes tax schedule viewing and task management. Pro (in-app purchase) adds the following:

  • Deadline reminder notifications (30/14/7/3/1 days before)
  • Full checklist display
  • PDF and CSV export, device calendar sync, home widget
  • Unlimited tax simulator
  • Multiple corporate profiles (up to 5)
  • View past fiscal years (5 years)

Pro is available as an annual or one-time purchase plan. You can purchase from "ZeiCal Pro" in Settings.

Q. Can I restore Pro purchased on another device?

Yes. Go to Settings → "ZeiCal Pro" → "Purchased on another device" → "Restore" to restore your previous purchase. You must be signed in with the same Apple ID / Google account as the App Store / Google Play.

Q. Where is my data stored?

Tax schedules and settings are stored only on your device. We do not send data to our servers. Communication occurs only during Pro purchase and restoration via the App Store / Google Play / RevenueCat for billing. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Q. Can I migrate data when changing devices?

The current version does not support data export/import. Pro purchase status can be transferred via "Restore," but tax data is stored on the device, so reconfiguration is required when changing devices.

Q. I am not receiving notifications

Reminder notifications are a Pro feature. If you have purchased Pro, check that notifications are ON in Settings and that notifications are allowed for ZeiCal under your device's Settings → Notifications.

Q. Can the tax simulator replace filing a tax return?

No. Simulator results are approximate estimates within about ±20%. They cannot be used to prepare returns or determine final tax amounts. Use them to get a general sense before filing. Consult a tax professional for details.

Q. Can I use the simulator in the free version?

Yes. The free version allows one use per year. Unlimited use requires Pro (annual or one-time purchase).

Q. I want to create invoices

Use our sister app Rakubill. It supports qualified invoices and offers a ¥500 one-time purchase for invoice issuance. You can also navigate to the store from ZeiCal Settings.
