Free No account required iOS / Android

Your closest line of defense on hot days

Five Ministry of the Environment levels — see risk at a glance.

Check before you go out

Walks, runs, shopping, commuting — whatever you're doing, checking WBGT before leaving helps lower heat stroke risk.

Thresholds tailored to you

Warning thresholds adjust for age group and medical history (optional). Shows WBGT guidelines by activity such as walking and running. Personalized thresholds are not medical advice.

Rainy season support too

In June–July, rainy season mode alerts you to indoor humidity and mold risk. Helps with health management in muggy seasons, not just summer heat.

Key features

Simple UI — anyone can use it right away.

🌡️

Real-time WBGT display

Semicircle gauge shows local heat index visually. Prioritizes Ministry of the Environment data.

📈

24-hour forecast

Graph of WBGT changes by hour. Spot dangerous time slots in advance.

🏃

Activity log

Record walks, runs, sports, and more. WBGT values and activity time are linked and saved.

📤

Share activity logs

Share records with photos. Share heat safety info with family and friends.

🚨

Danger level alerts

Local push notifications when configured thresholds are exceeded. Prevent missed warnings.

🎯

Activity recommendations

WBGT guidelines for walking, running, shopping, and more. Check recommended times in the 24-hour forecast.

👤

Personal profile

Adjust warning thresholds by age group and medical history (optional). Change anytime in onboarding or settings.

📱

Widget and Live Activity

Home screen widget shows current WBGT and danger level. On iOS, supports Live Activity while recording.

☔

Rainy season mode

Alerts for indoor humidity and mold risk. Health management during the rainy season.
