FAQ

Q. How accurate is the forecast?

It is based on 15-minute interval data from the Open-Meteo API. Sudden showers can change quickly, so use it as reference information only.

Q. I am not receiving notifications

Check that notifications are allowed for Shower Guard in your device's Settings app. Also confirm that "Rain Alert" is ON in the app settings.

Q. Is the forecast accurate?

Precipitation forecasts are for reference only and accuracy is not guaranteed. Sudden showers and similar rapid changes may not be covered. You are responsible for your final travel decisions.

Q. Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored only on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
