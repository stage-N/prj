FAQ

Q. What is the difference between the free version and Premium?

The free version allows up to 3 invoices and delivery notes per month. Estimates are unlimited, and payment due notifications are free. Premium (¥500 one-time purchase) adds:

  • Unlimited invoices and delivery notes
  • Unlimited sales CSV export (free version: once per month)
  • All free features including estimates, receipts, payment tracking, and notifications

Purchase from "Premium" in Settings. It is a one-time purchase, not a subscription.

Q. Can I restore Premium purchased on another device?

Yes. Go to Settings → "Premium" → "Restore Purchase" to restore your previous purchase. You must be signed in with the same Apple ID / Google account as the App Store / Google Play.

Q. Where is my data stored?

Client and invoice data are stored only on your device. We do not send data to our servers. Communication occurs only during Premium purchase and restoration via the App Store / Google Play for billing. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Q. Can I migrate data when changing devices?

Sales CSV export is supported (free version: once per month; Premium: unlimited). Bulk import between devices is not supported. Premium purchase status can be transferred via "Restore," but invoice data is stored on the device, so reconfiguration is required when changing devices.

Q. How do I export PDFs?

Select an invoice from the invoice list and share or save from the PDF preview screen. You can send it directly via email or chat apps.

Q. Does it support the invoice system?

Yes. You can register your qualified invoice issuer registration number in Settings, and it appears on invoices. Reduced tax rate (8%), standard rate (10%), and tax-exempt can be set per line item.

Q. How many estimates can I create?

Estimates are free and unlimited. You can convert an estimate to an invoice with one tap. Invoices and delivery notes are limited to 3 per month in the free version (unlimited with Premium).

Q. Can I use payment due notifications?

Yes. Available in the free version. Local notifications arrive 3 days before, on, and the day after the due date. You can turn them ON/OFF in Settings. Notifications are canceled when marked as paid.

Q. Can I record partial payments?

Yes. Enter the amount and date from "Payment Record" on the invoice detail screen. Full payment automatically marks the invoice as paid.

Q. Can I issue receipts?

You can issue receipt PDFs from paid invoices. You can also edit the description field. This does not consume quota.

Q. How do I use sales CSV?

Export sales CSV from Settings and share via email or the Files app. It is for manual import into accounting software such as freee, Money Forward, Yayoi, or for your tax advisor. There is no automatic sync. Free version: once per month; Premium: unlimited.

Q. I want to manage tax deadlines

Use our sister app ZeiCal to auto-generate tax schedules. You can also navigate to the store from Rakubill Settings.
