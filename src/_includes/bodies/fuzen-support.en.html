FAQ

Q. Why Fuzen when AI can code?

AI speeds up code generation. Fuzen is where you internalize decomposition, abstraction, and function composition with your own hands. The ability to read, fix, and correctly direct AI output comes from REPL practice. AI gives answers; thinking is yours.

Q. How do I use the REPL?

Open the app to see the Playground (REPL). Enter an expression and tap Run. Example: (+ 1 2) → 3

Q. What's free?

Playground (REPL) and Curriculum Phase 1–2 (10 lessons) are free. Phase 3–5 and My Library require Full unlock (one-time purchase).

Q. Can I restore Full unlock?

Yes. Tap "Restore purchase" in Settings. You must be signed in with the same Apple ID / Google account as the store.

Q. Do I need internet?

Learning and the REPL work offline. A connection may be needed temporarily when downloading app updates.

Q. Where is my data stored?

Progress, REPL history, and My Library are stored only on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
