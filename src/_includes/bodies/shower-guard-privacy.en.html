Privacy Policy

STAGEN ("we," "us," or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of personal information in Shower Guard (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App uses your device's location to obtain precipitation forecasts.

  • Location is used only to obtain precipitation forecasts
  • Coordinates are sent only when requesting weather data from the Open-Meteo API
  • No user registration is required
  • There is no account or login feature

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is not sent to our servers:

  • Notification settings (departure time, sensitivity)
  • Most recently fetched precipitation forecast data (for offline display)
  • Text for widget display

3. External Communications

  • Open-Meteo Weather API — Sends coordinates (latitude/longitude) to obtain precipitation forecasts

4. Notifications

The App provides local push notifications. Notifications are scheduled on your device; our servers are not involved. We hold APNs (Apple Push Notification service) credentials but do not send remote push from the server in the MVP version.

5. Advertising and Analytics

The App does not display ads or use analytics tools.

6. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose user data to third parties.

7. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App deletes all data on your device.

8. Disclaimer

Precipitation forecasts in the App are for reference only and accuracy is not guaranteed. You are responsible for your final travel decisions.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
