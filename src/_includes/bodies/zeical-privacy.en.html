Privacy Policy

STAGEN ("we," "us," or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of personal information in ZeiCal - Tax Calendar (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information for user registration or advertising purposes. Tax schedule settings are stored on your device only.

  • No account registration is required (except when purchasing Pro)
  • Tax calculations and schedule display work entirely offline
  • We do not use analytics tools or advertising SDKs

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only in local storage on your device and is not sent to our servers:

  • Fiscal year-end month and corporate profile settings
  • Prior fiscal year tax amount information
  • Task completion status
  • Notification settings
  • Widget display snapshots
  • Tax simulation history (up to 5 entries)

3. Pro (In-App Purchase)

Purchase and restoration of ZeiCal Pro are processed via RevenueCat and the App Store / Google Play. Only anonymous purchase information required by the store for billing is transmitted. We do not send your tax data to RevenueCat.

  • Internet connection is required to verify purchase status and restore purchases
  • You can view tax schedules for free without purchasing Pro

4. Notifications

As a Pro feature, the App provides local push notifications. Notifications are scheduled on your device; no external server is involved. Reminder notifications are not available in the free version.

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose tax data to third parties. For Pro purchases only, billing-related information is provided to Apple, Google, and RevenueCat.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be changed without prior notice. The revised policy takes effect when posted on this page.

8. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
