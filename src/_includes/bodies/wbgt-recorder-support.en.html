FAQ

Q. Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored on your device (SQLite) only. See the Privacy Policy.

Q. Where does WBGT data come from?

We use the Open-Meteo API and Ministry of the Environment heat index data, prioritizing the ministry data for display.

Q. I am not receiving notifications

Check that notifications are allowed under your device's Settings → Notifications → Heat Stroke Recorder Pro.

Q. What is the Lite plan?

A ¥3,000/month auto-renewing subscription. It includes WBGT monitoring, alerts, record storage, and CSV export.

Q. Can I restore my purchase?

From the paywall screen, tap "Restore" with the same Apple ID / Google account.

Q. Where can I export CSV / PDF?

From the Export tab, specify a period and export.

Q. Can I migrate data when changing devices?

Data export/import is not currently supported. Purchase status can be transferred via "Restore" only.
