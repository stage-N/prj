Privacy Policy

STAGEN ("we," "us," or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of personal information in Cool Walk (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App uses your device's location to obtain WBGT, UV, and air quality data.

  • Location is used only to obtain weather and air quality data
  • Coordinates are sent only when requesting data from the Open-Meteo API
  • No user registration is required
  • HealthKit and similar health data are not used in the MVP version

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is not sent to our servers:

  • Activity type and notification settings
  • Most recently fetched WBGT, UV, and PM forecast data (for offline display)

3. External Communications

  • Open-Meteo Forecast API — Weather data for WBGT estimation
  • Open-Meteo Air Quality API — PM2.5 / PM10 data
  • OpenStreetMap Overpass API — Park and green space data (shaded route recommendations)
  • OSRM — Walking route calculation (shaded route recommendations)
  • GSI map tiles — Map display

Only coordinates (latitude/longitude) are sent in each case.

4. Notifications

The App provides local push notifications. Recommended time-of-day notifications at 7 a.m. and WBGT warnings at 28°C or above are scheduled on your device.

5. Advertising and Analytics

The App does not display ads or use analytics tools.

6. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose user data to third parties.

7. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App deletes all data on your device.

8. Disclaimer

The App is not a medical device. Information provided is guidance for going outdoors and is not medical diagnosis or treatment advice. Consult a physician or other qualified professional for heat stroke prevention and health decisions.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
