Privacy Policy

STAGEN ("we," "us," or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of personal information in Heat Stroke Recorder Pro (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App uses your device's location to obtain WBGT (Wet Bulb Globe Temperature) values.

  • Location is used only to obtain WBGT values
  • Coordinates are sent only when requesting weather data from the Open-Meteo API
  • We do not collect personal information for user registration or advertising purposes

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device in a SQLite database and is not sent to our servers:

  • Work records (start/end times, work type, number of workers, etc.)
  • Settings such as company name and site name
  • Notification and threshold settings
  • Data linking WBGT values to records

We do not sync or back up data to external servers. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

3. External Communications

The App communicates with the following external services:

  • Open-Meteo API — Sends coordinates (latitude/longitude) to obtain weather data
  • Ministry of the Environment Heat Index site — Retrieves reference heat index data

We do not communicate with other external services except when purchasing or restoring the Lite plan (in-app purchase).

4. Lite Plan (In-App Purchase)

Purchase and restoration of the Lite plan are processed via RevenueCat and the App Store / Google Play. Only anonymous purchase information required by the store for billing is transmitted. We do not send your work records or WBGT data to RevenueCat.

5. Notifications

The App provides local push notifications. Notifications are scheduled on your device; no external server is involved.

6. Analytics and Advertising

The App does not use analytics tools or advertising SDKs.

7. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose work records or WBGT data to third parties. For billing only, billing-related information is provided to Apple, Google, and RevenueCat.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we revise this policy, we will announce the change in advance on GitHub.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
