FAQ

Q. What games are included?

Five games: Counting, Sequencing (Ordering), Drawing, Animals, and Tidying Up. Clearing games earns stars and unlocks new areas and companions (animal friends).

Q. How do I open Parent Settings?

Long-press the ⚙️ button on the map screen to open "Grown-Up Settings." You can manage play time, language (Japanese / English), sound, and view or reset progress.

Q. Can I play on iPad?

Yes. It supports both iPhone and iPad. The layout adjusts to screen size.

Q. Where is my data stored?

Everything is stored only on your device. No data is sent to external servers. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Q. Is an internet connection required?

Gameplay works offline. A connection may be needed temporarily only when downloading app updates.

Q. Are there ads or in-app purchases?

The App is ¥350 (tax included, one-time purchase) on the App Store and Google Play. There are no ads or additional in-app purchases. All features are included after purchase.
