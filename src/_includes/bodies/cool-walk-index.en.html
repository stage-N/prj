🌳 Shaded routes too

When you must go out now, the app automatically picks parks and green spaces within 1.5 km as destination candidates and shows a walking route under 15 minutes on the map. No manual destination entry. "Shade" means the share of the route passing within 40 m of parks or green space — not building shadows. Calculated with OpenStreetMap + OSRM.
