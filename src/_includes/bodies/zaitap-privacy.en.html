Privacy Policy

STAGEN ("we," "us," or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of personal information in ZaiTap and ZaiTap for Administrative Scriveners (collectively, the "Service").

1. Service Structure

The Service consists of two applications:

  • ZaiTap (Applicant) — For foreign residents. Input, submission, and progress tracking of residence procedures, and consultation requests to administrative scriveners
  • ZaiTap for Administrative Scriveners — B2B tool for partner scriveners. Receive consultation requests, review documents, and communicate

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we may process the following information:

  • Device identifiers (for session management and push notifications)
  • Input related to residence procedures (application XML, consultation summaries, etc.)
  • Encrypted attachments (when uploaded during consultation)
  • Fee estimate consent records (timestamp, procedure type, amount range)
  • Scrivener app: office name, device Store ID, subscription status

Account registration is not required for basic use of the applicant self-filing features (except registration for the scrivener app).

3. Purpose of Use

  • Submission on behalf of applicants to the Residence Application Procedures API (self-filing)
  • Mediation of consultation and communication with administrative scriveners
  • Sending push notifications
  • Displaying fee estimates and recording consent logs
  • Managing subscriptions and matching quotas for scriveners

4. Servers and Storage

For consultation and communication features only, data is processed on Cloudflare Workers (D1 database and R2 storage). Encrypted documents are stored in R2 and automatically deleted after a set period. Application XML generation and NFC signing are processed on the device.

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

  • Immigration Services Agency of Japan — Application data sent via API during self-filing
  • Partner administrative scriveners — Consultation information and encrypted documents within the scope agreed by the client after consultation request and acceptance
  • Expo Push — Notification delivery (tokens only)
  • RevenueCat / App Store / Google Play — Subscription and billing for the scrivener app only

The applicant app does not use advertising SDKs. We do not offer in-app purchases in the applicant app.

6. Note on the Administrative Scrivener Act

Download and submission features in the applicant app are free. We do not collect fees from applicants. Compensation to scriveners is based on a direct contract between the client and the scrivener. We provide referral, communication, and IT platform services only.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be changed without prior notice. The revised policy takes effect when posted on this page.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
