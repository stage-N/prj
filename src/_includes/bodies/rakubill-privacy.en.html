Privacy Policy

STAGEN ("we," "us," or "our") establishes this Privacy Policy regarding the handling of personal information in Rakubill (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information for user registration or advertising purposes. The following data is stored on your device only:

  • Company profile information (company name, address, qualified invoice issuer registration number, bank account, etc.)
  • Client information (company name, address, contact details, etc.)
  • Invoice data (items, amounts, tax, issue date, payment due date, etc.)

2. Data Storage Location

All of the above data is stored only on your device. We do not send any data to our servers. Invoice creation and viewing work offline.

3. Premium (In-App Purchase)

Purchase and restoration of the Premium version (¥500 one-time purchase) are processed via react-native-iap through the App Store / Google Play. Only anonymous purchase information required by the store for billing is transmitted. We do not send your invoice data to the store.

  • Internet connection is required to verify purchase status and restore purchases
  • The free version allows up to 3 invoices per month

4. Notifications

The App uses Expo Notifications for payment due date reminders. Notifications are scheduled on your device; invoice data and personal information are not sent to external servers. Notification tokens may be sent to Expo servers.

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose invoice data or client information to third parties. For Premium purchases only, billing-related information is provided to Apple and Google.

6. Data Deletion

All app data is stored on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be changed without prior notice. The revised policy takes effect when posted on this page.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please contact us via the Support page.
